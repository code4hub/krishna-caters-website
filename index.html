Our Story

Where Tradition Meets Every Table

Rooted in the rich culinary heritage of Kozhikode, Krishna Caters has been bringing the soul of Malabar cuisine to your celebrations since 2008. From the fragrant depths of our legendary Kozhikode Biryani to the divine spread of a traditional Sadya on banana leaf — every dish carries the care of a home kitchen at the scale of a grand feast.

Our team of seasoned chefs and service professionals have served over 5,000 events across Kozhikode, Malappuram, Wayanad, and beyond — from intimate gatherings of 50 to grand weddings of 5,000 guests.
